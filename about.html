☕ Our Story

🌱 Founded in 1971 in the historic Pike Place Market in Seattle, Washington, Starbucks began as a small store selling high-quality coffee beans and equipment. Inspired by the rich tradition of Italian espresso bars, Howard Schultz joined the company in the 1980s and transformed Starbucks into a coffeehouse where community and connection were just as important as coffee itself.

🌍 Over the decades, Starbucks has grown into a global phenomenon with over 35,000 stores worldwide. Each store is built to reflect the culture of its neighborhood, providing a warm and inviting environment where customers feel at home.

❤️ Our mission is simple but powerful: “To inspire and nurture the human spirit — one person, one cup, and one neighborhood at a time.”

🌿 Passion for Coffee

☕ At Starbucks, coffee is more than just a drink — it’s a passion. We source only the finest 100% Arabica beans, grown and harvested with care from sustainable farms in Latin America, Africa, and Asia.

🔥 Our expert roasters craft each batch to bring out the best flavor profile of every bean. Whether it’s a smooth Blonde Roast or a bold Espresso Roast, every cup is made with precision and love.

🌎 Commitment to Community

👩‍🌾 Starbucks supports local communities and coffee farmers through ethical sourcing programs like C.A.F.E. Practices. We also invest in youth, education, and sustainability initiatives around the world.

🌱 We’re committed to reducing our environmental impact by promoting reusable cups, eliminating plastic straws, and aiming for carbon neutrality.

📅 Fun Facts

  • 📍 Starbucks was named after a character in the novel "Moby Dick."
  • 💡 The first store didn’t sell coffee drinks — only beans and equipment!
  • 🇮🇹 Inspired by a trip to Milan, Starbucks brought espresso culture to the U.S.
  • 🌟 Starbucks Rewards has over 30 million active members.
